General Tips

  • You can opt-out of things you don't need in your tuition, see tuition breakdown above
  • Take advantage of tutoring resources on your campus, whether it's in the form of tutoring centres, office hours, there is plenty of help
  • Go to orientation events, it's the best place to meet other students that are also looking for friends and going through the same transition as you!
  • Create a schedule to keep track of events and deadlines
  • Join a club for something that interests you outside of your major of study so you can have fun breaks from time to time
