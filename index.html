HIGH SCHOOL RESOURCE

OUR GOAL

A new way to learn.

High School Resource was created to make high-school more accessible. It’s a struggle to look for help on homework on websites that require subscriptions and that are halfway blurred out!

On the HSR website you can find help on any topic you need, from homework help, to university transition tips and even some basic financial literacy. Check out our resources tab above.

With HSR, you’ll be equipped to tackle high-school and the transition to university!

OUR PLAN

Our mission

Our mission at HSR is to make high school more accesible to all students. We hope that school becomes a place that everyone feels included and equally represented.

Our vision

Our vision at HSR is to create a free online learning environment where students get the best resources. We help to grow this platform by encouraging students to contribute their resources too!
